Overall Interview Preparation for Cracking Tough Interviews

Explore the best platforms for practicing different skills to get ready for interviews!

1. Technical Skills & Coding Platforms

  • HackerRank - 10 Days of JavaScript - Best platform to practice coding challenges and algorithms.
  • LeetCode - Coding Challenges - Solve coding problems to prepare for technical interviews.
  • CodeWars - Code Challenges - Learn to code through engaging challenges.
  • CodinGame - Coding Games - Improve coding skills through games and challenges.

2. System Design & Architecture

  • Educative - Grokking the System Design Interview - Master system design with this course.
  • System Design Primer - Learn system design fundamentals.
  • Gaurav Sen - System Design Videos - Learn system design concepts with real-world examples.

3. Data Structures & Algorithms

  • GeeksforGeeks - Data Structures - Study data structures with in-depth explanations and examples.
  • Udemy - Data Structures & Algorithms Bootcamp - Free course to get started with DSA.
  • Coursera - Algorithms Specialization - Explore algorithmic techniques and data structures.

4. Behavioral & Soft Skills

  • Coursera - Business Communication - Develop your communication and leadership skills.
  • TED Talks - Inspirational talks on communication and leadership.
  • Udemy - Public Speaking - Free courses to boost your speaking skills.

5. Mock Interviews & Interview Practice

  • Pramp - Conduct free peer-to-peer mock interviews.
  • Interviewing.io - Mock technical interviews with engineers from top companies.
  • MockInterview.co - Online platform for mock technical interviews.

6. Resume & Career Resources

  • Resume.io - Free resume-building platform.
  • Canva - Resume Templates - Professional resume templates.
  • VisualCV - Create interactive resumes with ease.

Best Resume Tips

  • Tailor Your Resume: Customize your resume for the specific job you're applying to.
  • Showcase Achievements: Focus on measurable results, not just duties.
  • Keep it Concise: Limit your resume to one or two pages.
  • Highlight Skills: Include both technical and soft skills relevant to the job.
  • Proofread: Ensure there are no typos or grammatical errors in your resume.
